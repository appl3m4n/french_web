    What are Cookies?

    Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, smartphone) when you access our website. They contain information about your browsing activities and preferences.

    We use cookies, specifically from Google Analytics, to collect information about how visitors use our site. This information helps us analyze website traffic, improve user experience, and tailor our content to better meet the needs of our users.
